Cardiology

Welcome to our Cardiology Department, where heart health takes center stage.

Our Cardiology Department is dedicated to heart health, offering comprehensive cardiovascular care. From preventive measures to advanced treatments, our experienced cardiologists utilize cutting-edge technologies to diagnose and manage heart conditions, ensuring optimal cardiac well-being for our patients.

Neurology

Step into our Neurology Department, where the complexities of the nervous system are met with expertise and compassion.

At Reliance Hospital's Neurology Department, we focus on neurological health, addressing conditions affecting the brain and nervous system. From strokes to neurodegenerative disorders, our neurologists provide advanced care to support optimal neurological well-being.

Hepatology Department

Welcome to our Hepatology Department, where liver health is a priority.

The Hepatology Department at Reliance Hospital specializes in liver care. Our hepatologists provide comprehensive treatment options for liver diseases, emphasizing preventive measures and personalized guidance for maintaining liver health.

Pediatrics Department

Step into our Pediatrics Department, where the health and happiness of your little ones are our primary concerns.

At Reliance Hospital's Pediatrics Department, we prioritize the unique healthcare needs of young patients. Our pediatricians offer a range of services, from routine check-ups to vaccinations, fostering the health and well-being of children at every stage.

Eye Care Department

Enter our Eye Care Department, where vision is cherished, and eye health is our focus.

The Eye Care Department at Reliance Hospital specializes in comprehensive eye care. Our ophthalmologists offer services ranging from routine eye examinations to advanced surgical interventions, promoting eye health and ensuring personalized solutions for various eye conditions.
